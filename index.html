Protocol

How It Works

Four steps. Arm it, leave it, let them try.

// 01
🔑

Set Your PIN

Choose a 4-digit PIN on first launch. This is the only way to stop the alarm — no way around it.

// 02
📴

Place & Arm

Tap Start Protection. A countdown gives you time to set the phone down and step away.

// 03
📳

Threat Detected

The accelerometer and gyroscope detect any movement. The alarm fires instantly — loud, persistent, unstoppable.

// 04
📸

Intruder Logged

Front camera captures the intruder the moment they try to enter a PIN. Logged with timestamp and trigger type.

Capabilities

Built to Protect

Every feature exists for one reason — making it impossible to touch your phone undetected.

🚨

Instant Alarm

Loud, looping alarm the moment motion is detected. Vibration + flashlight strobe simultaneously. Impossible to ignore.

Core Feature
📳

Smart Motion Detection

Accelerometer + gyroscope with auto-calibration and rolling-average smoothing. No false triggers from vibrating surfaces.

📸

Intruder Selfie

Front camera fires when someone tries to enter a PIN. Wrong PIN? Another photo. Every attempt is documented.

🔐

PIN + Biometric Lock

Only your 4-digit PIN or fingerprint stops the alarm. Back button does nothing. Home button does nothing.

🌑

Background Monitoring

Android foreground service keeps sensors alive when the screen is off. Alarm fires even when the phone is locked.

📊

Intruder Log

Full audit trail. Timestamp, trigger type, intruder photo — every incident recorded and viewable anytime.

Response System

What Happens
When They Touch It

  • Alarm fires in <100ms Motion is detected, debounced, and the alarm starts before they can react.
  • Vibration + Flashlight Alternating haptic pattern and 300ms torch strobe. Maximum attention.
  • Wrong PIN = Photo taken Every failed attempt captures a front-facing photo and increments the attempt counter.
  • Correct PIN = Disarmed Alarm stops, status resets to idle. The log remains for review.
